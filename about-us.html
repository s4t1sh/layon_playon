Welcome to Lay On 'N' Play On

How we began

Lay On ‘N’ Play On a unit of AJ Enterprise was established in 2016 by Arundathi and Jeevan, a playground specialist company in Bangalore. Over the years, our team has expanded to cover all of India and service offering has grown substantially to better serve our clients and communities we proudly support.


About us

Our playground equipment ideas has been with us since the beginning. Our thoughtful approach, unparalleled quality and innovative designs that includes safety standards. We share an unwavering commitment to proving the best products, service and results for our clients.


We are dedicated to providing our clients with memorable play experiences that are safe, engaging and built to last. The playground equipment we install are of age and developmentally appropriate, sensory stimulating and universally designed. Our design philosophy creates play spaces that are more accessible and appealing for everyone.


We can work together to design an exciting and efficient playground that is safe, fun and comfortable for all to enjoy.


Bring your ideas to Lay On ‘N’ Play On and we’ll create something amazing for you!
